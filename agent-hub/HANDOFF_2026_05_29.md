## The One Hard-Won Lesson

**sqflite_sqlcipher version compatibility matrix:**
| Version | CI compatibility | Reason |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| ≤3.1.0+1 | ✅ Works | Fixed compileSdkVersion in build.gradle |
| 3.2.0 | ❌ Gradle breaks | Introduced `flutter.compileSdkVersion` — not available in LibraryExtension on Flutter 3.22 |
| 3.2.1+ | ❌ Pub fails | Requires `flutter: '>=3.27.0'` — CI uses Flutter 3.22 |

**Never change `sqflite_sqlcipher: 3.1.0+1` without first upgrading CI to Flutter 3.27+.**
