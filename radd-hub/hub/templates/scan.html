Paste cookies from browser DevTools

ACTION REQUIRED
Alternative to OTP — log in to cloud.jazzdrive.com.pk in your browser, open DevTools → Application → Cookies and paste the two values below.
How to get cookies (step-by-step)
  1. Open cloud.jazzdrive.com.pk in Chrome or Edge on your phone/PC.
  2. Log in with your Jazz mobile number — you'll receive an SMS OTP.
  3. After login, press F12 (or right-click → Inspect).
  4. Go to Application tab → Cookies → https://cloud.jazzdrive.com.pk.
  5. Find validation_key and JSESSIONID — copy each value and paste above.
